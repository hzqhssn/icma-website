Our Services

Our Services

We are a small and vibrant musallah dedicated to serving the needs of our local Muslim community. Our aim is to provide a peaceful and inclusive space for worship, education, and community engagement. Here are the services we aim to offer:

Daily Prayers: Due to the current restrictions, we are unable to offer daily Salah.

Friday Prayers (Jumu'ah): Join us every Friday for congregational prayers and inspiring sermons (khutbah) delivered by knowledgeable speakers.

Ramadan Services: During the blessed month of Ramadan, we host Taraweeh prayers and provide a spiritually uplifting atmosphere for our community to engage in extra acts of worship.

Islamic Education: Our musallah offers educational programs to enhance your knowledge of Islam. Join our classes on Quranic recitation, Arabic language, Islamic history, and the teachings of our beloved Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).

Community Events: We foster a sense of togetherness through various community gatherings. Join us for iftars during Ramadan, Eid celebrations, lectures, workshops, and other engaging events.

Our Mission

our mission is to provide a welcoming and nurturing space for Muslims to connect with Allah through prayer, foster a sense of community, and promote a deeper understanding of Islam.
